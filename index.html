About Me

I’ve spent the last 10 years working in a range of digital roles, helping all sorts of organisations to boost online presence and make efficiencies with their digital processes. My experience has taught me about the broad range of marketing challenges faced by businesses every day, which continue to grow as the digital landscape evolves.

As a consultant I can help demystify the path to success in today’s highly-competitive online environment. I keep up to date with digital trends and technologies, regularly checking in with industry-leading resources like The Digital Marketing Podcast, ProductHunt and SearchEngineLand. This allows me to recommend the most appropriate tools and strategies to clients and help them stand out from the crowd. Read more Read less

With my analytics background, I use data to drive decision-making and ensure results are quantifiable. Prior to consulting, I helped theatre ticketing website SeatPlan to triple monthly traffic over two years through SEO. At AccorHotels I oversaw their UK PPC campaigns, ensuring they remained profitable to the business throughout my employment. More recently, I’ve worked with Hilton to build out internal mass communication workflows and marketing performance dashboards. These are the kind of results and outputs I work hard to deliver for each of my employers.

If you are a growth-focused organisation, please do get in touch to learn more about my experience and find out how I can help you.
