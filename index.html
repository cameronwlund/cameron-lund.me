I developed most of my SEO knowledge working for a small online theatre ticket affiliate called SeatPlan. In a highly competitive environment I worked with an SEO expert to rank the website for valuable keywords, tripling organic traffic over two years with the following tactics.

  • Technical SEO: Using spidering tools like Screaming Frog to identify crawl issues in the site's HTML.

  • Outreach: Analysing the web for backlink opportunities with SEO tools like Ahrefs and putting together a robust link building strategy using BuzzStream.

  • Content Strategy: Conducting keyword analysis and identifying content opportunities that will increase keyword coverage and ultimately provide benefit to users, increasing traffic and backlinks.

I can conduct a full audit of your website using the above tactics and tools to help boost your organic rank across search engines.
